Describes how community foundations use digital forms across six grantmaking stages: LOI intake, grant applications, site visit coordination, decision documentation, agreement execution, and reporting workflows. Community foundations manage grantmaking for donor-advised and field-of-interest funds with a higher volume of grants per staff member than most private foundations.
